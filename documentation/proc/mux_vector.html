public function mux_vector(v, mesh, base)

Multiplex index of a single wave vector. v is the demultiplexed triplet of a wave vector. i is the multiplexed index of a wave vector (always 1-based). mesh is the number of wave vectors along the three reciprocal lattice vectors. base states whether v has 0- or 1-based indexing.
