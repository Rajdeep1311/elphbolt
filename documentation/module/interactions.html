public subroutine calculate_3ph_interaction(ph, crys, num, key)

Parallel driver of the 3-ph vertex calculator for all IBZ phonon wave vectors. This subroutine calculates |V-(s1|s2q2,s3q3)|^2, W-(s1|s2q2,s3q3), and W+(s1|s2q2,s3q3) for each irreducible phonon and saves the results to disk.
